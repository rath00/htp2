讓我們來畫個簡單的圖吧！

請用鉛筆在一張白紙或是下頁面的繪圖板上任意畫一幅包括房子、樹木和人物在內的畫。 想怎麼畫就怎麼畫，不過你要認真地畫，不要畫火柴人喔！ 另外，請不要用尺、橡皮擦等其他輔助的工具，可以重畫同樣的物件，但不要塗改線條喔！ 在時間方面請在五分鐘之內完成構思，並在十分鐘之內完成畫作吧～

Please use a pen to draw any drawing including a house, trees and people on a blank sheet of paper or the drawing board on the next page. Draw whatever you want, but you have to draw carefully, not stick figures! In addition, please do not use rulers, erasers and other auxiliary tools. You can redraw the same objects, but do not modify the lines! In terms of time, please complete the idea within five minutes and complete the painting within ten minutes~
